In any case, Chinese culture is a little different than western  
culture in some senses. They prefer to deal with people that they know  
on a personal basis. Rather than through a machine.

PreisShare is a facilitator for sharing the pricing/product  
information. And being able to retrieve it from a local resident  
product database.

Asians especially want 'good price - very quick'. That's what  
preisshare is oriented towards. Waiting for a bid to come back may not  
be quick enough. But I may be wrong.
