Hi Steve,

I've decided that we should make it as easy as possible for ourselves.

Therefore, I'm thinking that we could try distributing the 'Supplier  
product database.mdb' directly to suppliers.

Telling them to enter their product information into it.

Within access, it's pretty easy to add a few simple forms/data-entry screens.

Then what we can do is collect all the data and merge it into one  
'big' database and redistribute it.

I think this could work. We should at least give it a go for the next  
six weeks.

This will be easier than spreadsheets. I think people will get a  
better feel for the advantages of using a databased solution.
